Streaming services

  • Steam streaming: Best option to play over LAN with Steam Controller support.
  • Nvidia Geforce Now: Just play, but forget to play already own games :(
  • LiquidSky: Best server renting.
  • Parsec: Best GNU/Linux support.
  • Vortex: Kind of Nvidia Geforce Now but allowing you to play your steam games but limited to its library. It supports linux using Chromium browser :D (There’re Android and Win clients too)
    • remotrapp: Streaming server and client (Android and Win) from the creators of Vortex

Steam Controller

  • sc-controller - User-mode driver and GTK3 based GUI for Steam Controller.
  • GloSC - Tool for using the Steam-Controller as systemwide XInput controller alongside a global overlay https://alia5.github.io/GloSC/
