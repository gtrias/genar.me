Buying land instructions

Specific Steps for Buying Land

Note: there are variations on how to do this, but this is about as simple of a path as possible:

You have four ways of buying land in Decentraland

  • Buy land at auction near the center of the world, called Genesis City. This land could go for substantially more than 1000 Mana per Land. The initial Auction will be held sometime in or after late December 2017.
  • Buy land in a “District” — land further away from the center of the world, on the ‘outskirts’. This land is 1000 Mana per land. The initial “staking” of land for Districts runs until about December 14, 2017. Many districts are “pooling their land”, so your investment is a “share” in the district, not specific land. READ THE DISTRICT PROPOSAL for a district before you invest in it, and participate in conversations in chat to learn more from the district leaders.
  • It is expected that you will be able to buy Land at 1000 Mana per Land after the districts are initially formed, at the ‘outskirts’ of the world, after initial district formation and the Genesis city auction, but the exact methodology isn’t set yet as of early December 2017.
  • Buy Land from an existing Landowner. (There is no marketplace set up for that yet as of December 2017)

To learn more about the Districts, see the District Proposals and join the public channels for the districts in Chat.
Determine how much Mana you want to get to buy land or to participate in auctions.
Use one of the market pricing web tools to determine how much ETH (Ethereum Ethers) you will need to buy to exchange for the Mana. Get some extra ETH for fees, like an extra 0.3 ETH should do.
One example of such a tool is [1]
Convert a fiat currency (such as Dollars or Euros) into Ethers, using an exchange that supports fiat conversion, such as using an account you set up at CoinBase.com
Don’t be surprised that all the legitimate exchanges require some serious proofs of your identity.
Get an Ethereum personal wallet.
Recommended is MetaMask.io, but you will probably also eventually have reason to use MyEtherWallet.com (be very careful not to get a scam lookalike from a similar scam URL, this is a very common way to lose crypto)
Both of these wallets are actually Chrome browser extensions
Be sure to record any secret keys, phrases and passwords in a very safe, secure, and secret place.
Transfer the Ethers into your personal wallet.
Open an account at one of the exchanges where you can buy Decentraland Mana using Ethers, such as Bittrex.com, Binance.com, or Liqui.io
You can see the current list of Mana exchanges at https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/decentraland/#markets
Transfer Ethers at least sufficient to buy the land you want into the exchange, transfer perhaps 0.1 ETH extra for fees in the exchange, but also leave at least 0.1 ETH in your MetaMask wallet for later fees.
Exchange your Ethers for Mana in that exchange
Transfer the Mana back to your MetaMask wallet address.
If you already have Mana in your MyEtherWallet, here is a tutorial about how to transfer those to MetaMask: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gBNfs3r8QsI&feature=youtu.be
While logged into your MetaMask wallet Chrome extension, go to the Decentraland “Terraform” page, also known as the Terraform dApp.
If you are not logged into your MetaMask wallet, you will get an error “We could not retrieve your account’s information.” Log into MetMask Chrome extension and then try loading that page again.
You have two ways of buying land at the Terraform page through Dec 14 2017:
Committing Mana for participating in the Genesis City Auction — for buying Lands near the center of the world, in an auction (price may be substantially more than 1000 Mana per Land in an auction).
Here is a tutorial: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RxwLlQfcTxo
There are announcements about the Dec 15, 1 week Terraforming event in the Decentraland Blog
